Welcome to AVCOE

Amrutvahini College of Engineering (AVCOE), Sangamner, established in 1983, is committed to excellence in engineering education. Accredited by NBA and NAAC, ISO 9001:2015 certified, with world-class IT infrastructure and 100% placement assistance.

VISION

To create opportunities for rural students to become capable engineers through continuous excellence in education.

MISSION

To produce disciplined, capable, and ethical technocrats who can tackle technological challenges for the betterment of society and the environment.
